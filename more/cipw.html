CI PRIVATE WEALTH

BRIEF

I was tasked with creating a new experience for the visitors of this site. A hub of sorts where users could navigate through articles, podcasts, webinars, etc. The main keywords were formal, clean, and familiar.

PROCESS

I knew I had to keep the user experience similar and consistent to the rest of the site - and that’s exactly what I did. I followed the branding strictly while ensuring that the user experience was seamless. I created a wireframe. Once that was approved, i moved on to a higher fidelity wireframe.

I looked at similar experiences on other CI sites and drew inspiration from them. I included the navigation, so the user could easily view articles/webinars/podcasts/videos without feeling like they were leaving the page which created a much better user experience. Once the mockup was approved, I completed the final design.

I also ensured that any text on the page passed a contrast test for accessibility reasons.

Wireframes and final designs were achieved in Adobe XD
