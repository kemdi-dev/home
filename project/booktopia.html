Project Summary

Booktopia

intro

Booktopia is an app that helps people easily find and save books. In addition to the app, there is also a website.

Inspired by justwatch.com where you can find where to stream or buy tv shows and movies.

Note: This is a mockup idea or personal project

Role

UI Design
Graphic Design
Research
Illustration

Colours

  • #8684ff
  • #222155
  • #f2f1ff

Process

By browsing competitors I was able to see what worked and what didn’t. I took notes of the positives and what would make for a better user experience. I was able to build out a user journey based on my notes. Then I began working on wireframes

After deciding on what part of the layout worked best, I made a high fidelity mockup and chose the colour palette. Initial sketches were done in Procreate. Final designs were completed in Figma.
