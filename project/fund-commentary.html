Major changes

  • Using a tile format instead of a standard list modernizes the page and makes it easier to navigate
  • The filters for “quarter” and “year” have been removed. Previously, every commentary ever uploaded was visible on this page so those filters were necessary but now we only have about 14 displayed. Once a newer commentary is uploaded, the previous one is removed from the page.
  • “Fund commentary” has also been removed from the commentary title since that’s also redundant, i.e. CI Canadian REIT ETF – Fund Commentary – June 2024
  • I decided to leave the month/year as part of the fund title because it’s important. Sometimes we can have a commentary from May and another commentary from June posted on the page at the same time – depending on when we receive the PDFs. You can see an example of this in the screenshot below:

There’s a commentary from May and another from March because we haven’t received the newer versions yet.

1. Using the naming convention of the pdf to auto-populate the fund commentary page

An example of the naming system currently being used:

(English) CI-Munro-Global-Growth-Equity-Fund-June2024.pdf (French) Fonds-d'actions-mondiales-axées-sur-la-croissance-CI-Munro-Juin2024.pdf - The name of this fund in English is “CI Munro Global Growth Equity Fund” and the name in french is “Fonds d'actions mondiales axées sur la croissance CI Munro”

Things to consider:

I. The team filter/tag. There are 6 in total

  • Alternatives
  • Auspice Capital Advisors
  • ETF
  • Marret Asset Management
  • Munro Partners
  • Sector Equity
  • **These teams are added through “Tagging” on AEM: [redacted link]

The naming convention can be updated if needed. Acronoyms that indicate the different teams can be added to the end of the pdf. Similar to this:
(EN) CI-Munro-Global-Growth-Equity-Fund-June2024-MP.pdf
(FR) Fonds-d'actions-mondiales-axées-sur-la-croissance-CI-Munro-Juin2024 -MP.pdf
Here, MP stands for “Munro Partners”

We can use different acronyms to identify the teams:

  • AL – Alternatives
  • AC - Auspice capital advisors
  • ET - ETF
  • MA - Marret asset management
  • MP - Munro partners
  • SE - Sector equity
  • **Note that the team names are not translated to French. They are the same in English and French

New component design

Benefits:

  • The naming convention of the PDFs won’t matter.
  • This will significantly cut down on the time needed to upload and remove multiple commentaries. We’ll be able to add several commentaries at the same time rather than having to create an article page for ~every~ fund commentary. This also means less “dummy” pages in our repo.
  • With this component, we’ll also be able to arrange the commentaries however we please.

UI/UX notes:

  • Path to commentary links to the PDF
  • The fund title is displayed on the tile, i.e. if the fund title is “CI Enhanced Government Bond ETF – June 2024” and Marret Asset Management is selected under “Team” then this is how the tile will be displayed:
  • Team should have the 6 different options
  • Accent colour refers to the colour of the download icon and the colour of the team name. The default should be #3e71aa which is being used on the current fund commentary page. There should be three other choices: sapphire, black, white.
  • The colour choices for tile background colour should be the same as the background colours on the container component: none, black, white, sapphire, slate, gold, link, graphite, platinum, grey, burgundy, cobalt. “white” should be the default colour.
  • Tile outline refers to the drop shadow. There should be two options: drop shadow and none. “Drop shadow” should be the default.
  • The default fund title style should be the same paragraph styling that’s currently being used on the fund commentary page. The other font styles should be available as a choice, i.e. Headline 1 – Dark, Headline 1 – Light, Headline 2 – Dark, Headline 2 – Light, etc.
