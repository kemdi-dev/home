Project Summary

Mitto

intro

I was tasked with designing and enhancing the experience of multiple pages on the Motto site. Pages were supposed to attract a more youthful audience; a fun environment.

Role

Design
Research
Front-end development

Colours

  • #eaeef1
  • #5f2b7d
  • #d43b77

Process

Most of these were new experiences that didn’t exist on the site yet, which meant a lot of initial research had to be conducted. I was given some old user journeys and survey data. I was also given a list of competitors and I observed each site to note down what they’d done similarly and why.

  • What are the similarities across competitors?
  • Why were these features implemented?
  • Will this experience be useful on the Mitto site?

After the research stage, I made a few user journeys based on existing data, and taking into account the different experiences. I started with a lo-fi mockup then moved to a higher fidelity mockup once I felt happy with the results.
